About Hornsby Pet Shop

As well as rehoming and sheltering many different kinds of animals, Hornsby Pet Shelter also provides different equipment that is necessary for raising and caring for pets. The pet store has been run by locals since it opened in 2007. Our profits go towards running the shelter as well as other Sydney based animal charities. We make it easy for you to shop for all your pet's needs from dogs to cats, wet food and dry food, toys and treats.

Our whole team has a passion for pets and we are always here to help! We can answer your questions about which products are the most suited to your pet and give you tips about making sure your pet/s have the best lifestyle possible. We have many reputable brands available including Hill's Science Diet, Ivory Coat, Black Hawk and many others!
