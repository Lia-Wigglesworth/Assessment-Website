Adopting a Pet!

To start the adoption process for your potential pet, please fill out the form. Please consider your decision carefully before you make this decision. If you are approved for adoption, you will be allocated an appointment to meet the pet/s you selected and to process the adoption. If this is successful you will begin a 2 week trial period. The trial is organised to ensure the pet is right for you. Hornsby Pet Shelter reserves the right to terminate the trial at any given moment, and until the end of the adoption trial, the pet is still owned by Hornsby Pet Shelter. Transfer of ownership will only happen if the 2 week trial is successful. Please fill out the form truthfully and in as much detail as possible. Our team will get in contact with you as soon as possible
