Description

This alcohol-free ear cleanser contains antimicrobial actives which deal with several problems associated with ears as well as being an ideal ear maintenance product. The regular use of Epi-Otic aids in the prevention and treatment of ear infections by cleaning moist, waxy and smelly ears. Aiding in the treatment of otitis externa, Epi-Otic is a valuable therapeutic option for your cat or dog. The useful cleaning and preventative agent removes cerumen, pus and debris from the ear canal. It contains a drying agent to remove excess moisture, making it great to use after swims. Epi-Otic contains 4 broad-spectrum antimicrobials, treating and protecting against many infections. It has proven in vitro and in vivo efficacy against the major ear pathogens including Staphylococcus intermedius, Pseudomonas aeruginosa and Proteus spp.

Additional information

Ingredients Water, propylene glycol, lactic acid, Spherulites, docusate sodium, salicylic acid, PCMX, dimethicone, fragrance and FD&C blue #1. Lactic acid and salicylic acid are present in encapsulated (SPHERULITES) and free forms. Chitosanide is present in encapsulated form.
