Description

This alcohol-free ear cleanser contains antimicrobial actives which deal with several problems associated with ears as well as being an ideal ear maintenance product. The regular use of Epi-Otic aids in the prevention and treatment of ear infections by cleaning moist, waxy and smelly ears. Aiding in the treatment of otitis externa, Epi-Otic is a valuable therapeutic option for your cat or dog. It has an ideal pH of 2.5, creating a hostile environment for bacteria and yeast. The useful cleaning and preventative agent removes cerumen, pus and debris from the ear canal. It contains a drying agent to remove excess moisture, making it great to use after swims and leaves the ears in optimum condition. Epi-Otic contains 4 broad-spectrum antimicrobials, treating and protecting against a broad range of infections. The actives are anti-bacterial, keratin modifying, cerumenolytic, anti-pruritic and moisturising. It has proven in vitro and in vivo efficacy against the major ear pathogens including Staphylococcus intermedius, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, Proteus spp and Malassezia pachydermatis. Note: It is recommended that you conduct a skin test before use in case your pet is allergic to it
