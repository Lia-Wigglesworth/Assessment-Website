Description

Veterinarian approved Eye Wipes provide the most convenient way to keep your pet's eye area clean and healthy. The natural formula on these specialized pet wipes gently removes dirt and discharge and reduces tear stains that are found around the eyes to help prevent irritation, infection and to keep your pets appearance neat. It removes dirt & tear stains, Helps prevent eye infection, is non-toxic, natural and extra moist. These are particularly important for breeds with lots of wrinkles around their eyes as these can easily become infected.
