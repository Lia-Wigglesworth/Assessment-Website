Description

The JW GripSoft Cat Nail Clipper is a part of the JW feline only grooming line. This nail clipper is just the right size to safely and easily trim your cat's nails. The non-slip comfortable rubber handle makes it easier to hold the clipper providing a safer grooming experience for your pet. This is used and advocated by many veterinary professionals as it can provide comfort for the animal as well as ensuring that it is as easy as possible for the owner. It is important for pet and owner that normal scissors or nail clippers are not used as this could damage the cats nails.
