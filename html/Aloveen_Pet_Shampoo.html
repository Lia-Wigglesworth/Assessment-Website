Description

Aloveen Shampoo is a moisturising, lightly fragranced shampoo which can be used at any age on dogs, puppies, cats and kittens. Its oatmeal formula leaves your pets coat feeling replenished and clean. Dogs and cats with normal skin will benefit from clean skin and fur thanks to the gently foaming shampoo base, the soap-free pH balanced formula that allows them to retain their natural coat oils and a glossy coat. Aloveen is also ideal for pets who often suffer from dry and itchy skin as the formula can drastically reduce their discomfort through its soothing ingredients. Aloveen is designed to be left on the coat for 5 minutes to allow the properties to take effect.

Additional information

Ingredients 2% Oatmeal Extract - A stabilised suspension of superfine colloidal Oatmeal, renowned for its soothing, anti-itch properties Aloveen Also Contains: An emollient and cooling Aloe Vera gel.
