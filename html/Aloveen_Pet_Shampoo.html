Description

Aloveen Shampoo and Aloveen Intensive Conditioner can be used on any dog or cat that enjoys a moisturising, lightly fragranced shampoo, and helps those who suffer from dry and itchy skin. The popular Aloveen range can be used at any age on dogs, puppies, cats and kittens. Its oatmeal formula ensures a soothing effect and leaves your pets coat feeling replenished and clean. Dogs and cats with normal skin will benefit from clean skin and fur thanks to the gently foaming shampoo base, the soap-free pH balanced formula that allows them to retain their natural coat oils and a glossy coat thanks to the inclusion of emollients and moisturisers. Aloveen is also ideal for dog and cats with allergies who often suffer from dry and itchy skin. The Aloveen formula can drastically reduce your pets discomfort through its soothing ingredients. Aloveen is designed to be left on the coat for 5 minutes to allow the soothing properties of oatmeal to take effect.
