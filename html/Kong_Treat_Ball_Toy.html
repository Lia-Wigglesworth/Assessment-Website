Description

Place treats inside and watch your cat chase and swat the Kong Treat Ball! This will provide physical exercise and mental stimulation for your cat as they try to figure out how to get the treats at, as well as enjoyment for them. The unpredictable movement creates a new experience for them every time and makes it very challenging and stimulating. It required minimal effort from the owners, so you can just watch your cat have fun! This toy will help to keep your cat happy. See our range pet treats for those that you can place inside. You can also put kibble or pieces of meat inside but be careful as if they stay in there for too long they could go off.
