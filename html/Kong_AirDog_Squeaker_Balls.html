Description

The Kong SqueakAir Ball combines two Classic dog toys - the tennis ball and the squeaker toy - to create the perfect fetch toy that engages many different dogs through multiple of their different senses including sight and sound. The durable, high-quality SqueakAir tennis ball will not wear down your dog's teeth. It's made with a special non-abrasive felt, compared to the abrasive material on normal tennis balls, so your dogs enamel is protected. The fabric used for these balls is also tougher than tennis balls so it will last for longer and be able to withstand larger dog breeds.
