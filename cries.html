About Us

So you're interested in owning a cat? While the cat vs dog debate continues to be argued, there's no question that cats are easier to take care of, are less of a nuisance, are less expensive, and overall adorable. The comedians of the pet world, the playful and curious antics of the cats will always have you laughing. Even simple things such as a cardboard box or scrunched up paper ball can provide hours of amusement. If you are out all day at work or live in an apartment, cats could be perfect for you as they can look after themselves and are perfectly suited to an apartment or unit. Many of the cats that are available for adoption at Hornsby Pet Shelter, will have to be indoor pets for the rest of their lives due to FIV or cat flu. There are also meticulously clean and spend a lot of time grooming themselves and figure out how to use a litter box fairly quickly.

Despite the misunderstanding that all cats are aloof and unaffectionate, all cats have different personalities and traits. Each individual is full of character. There are cats suited to the hustle of family life and also those who prefer a peaceful life.It isn't only dogs that can be trained as well. Cats are very intelligent and through positive reinforcement training and some tasty treats they can be taught to come, sit, play fetch and other things.

How to Look After a Cat

There are many steps that you have to take to ensure that your cat has a comfortable environment and is cared for properly. This will differ between each cat and we highly recommend contacting the Hornsby Pet Shelter team members to ask for advice. Though there are requirement that remain relatively constant for all cats:

  • Provide plenty of human companionship
  • Provide regular, suitable meals with a constant supply of fresh water
  • Provide a clean and comfortable bed
  • Provide the cat with outdoor access or be prepared to empty and clean a litter tray on a daily basis
  • Provide it with a stimulating and safe environment
  • Groom it regularly. One of the benefits of short-haired cats is that they don't need to be groomed as frequently. It is recommended to groom short haired cats around once a week while medium or long haired cats should be groomed daily
  • Vaccinate against the major feline diseases regularly
  • Worm regularly and provide treatment for fleas
  • Take the cat to the vet when it shows any sign of illness
